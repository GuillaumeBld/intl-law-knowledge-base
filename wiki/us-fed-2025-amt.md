# Alternative Minimum Tax — US Federal, Tax Year 2025

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Tax year:** 2025

The AMT is a parallel income tax ensuring high-income filers pay a minimum amount regardless of deductions and preferences. Exemption reduces AMT income; exemption phases out above a threshold.

| Filing status | Exemption | Phase-out begins |
|---|---:|---:|
| Unmarried (single) | $88,100 | $626,350 |
| Married filing separately | $68,650 | — |
| Married filing jointly | $137,000 | $1,252,700 |
