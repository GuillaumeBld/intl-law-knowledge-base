# Standard Deduction — US Federal, Tax Year 2025

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Tax year:** 2025 · **Effective for returns filed in:** 2026

Taxpayers who do not itemize deduct a fixed amount from AGI based on filing status.

| Filing status | 2025 amount | Change vs 2024 |
|---|---:|---:|
| Single / Married filing separately | $15,000 | +$400 |
| Married filing jointly | $30,000 | +$800 |
| Head of household | $22,500 | +$600 |

Structured data: `data/us-fed-2025-key-amounts.csv` (rows where `item=standard_deduction`).
