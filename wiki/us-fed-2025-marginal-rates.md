# Marginal Income Tax Rates — US Federal, Tax Year 2025

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Tax year:** 2025

Seven-bracket progressive schedule. Bracket floors are taxable-income thresholds (AGI minus deductions).

## Single filer

| Rate | Taxable income over |
|---:|---:|
| 10% | $0 |
| 12% | $11,925 |
| 22% | $48,475 |
| 24% | $103,350 |
| 32% | $197,300 |
| 35% | $250,525 |
| 37% | $626,350 |

Structured data: `data/us-fed-2025-brackets.csv`.

## Worked example
A single filer with taxable income of $60,000 pays:
- 10% × $11,925 = $1,192.50
- 12% × ($48,475 − $11,925) = $4,386.00
- 22% × ($60,000 − $48,475) = $2,535.50
- **Total: $8,114.00** (effective rate ≈ 13.5%)
