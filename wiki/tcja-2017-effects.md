# TCJA (2017) — Provisions Still Shaping 2025

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Act:** Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, P.L. 115-97 (Dec. 22, 2017)

The Tax Cuts and Jobs Act made structural changes still governing tax year 2025:

- **Personal exemption eliminated** → $0 for 2025 (see [[us-fed-2025-unchanged-items]])
- **Overall limitation on itemized deductions (Pease) eliminated** → no cap for 2025
- **Standard deduction roughly doubled** (pre-TCJA baselines), still reflected in 2025 amounts (see [[us-fed-2025-standard-deduction]])
