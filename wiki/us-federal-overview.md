# US Federal Tax — Overview

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Authority:** Internal Revenue Service (IRS) · **Primary statute:** Internal Revenue Code (Title 26 U.S.C.)

The US federal income tax is administered by the IRS under Title 26 of the U.S. Code. Each year the IRS issues a Revenue Procedure adjusting more than 60 provisions for inflation. For tax year 2025, that guidance is **Revenue Procedure 2024-40** (announced in IR-2024-273, Oct. 22, 2024). Amounts in this KB for tax year 2025 flow from that release.

## Structure
- **Rates:** progressive, seven brackets — see [[us-fed-2025-marginal-rates]]
- **Baseline deduction:** standard deduction by filing status — see [[us-fed-2025-standard-deduction]]
- **Parallel system:** Alternative Minimum Tax — see [[us-fed-2025-amt]]
- **Credits/exclusions:** EITC, adoption, foreign earned income — see [[us-fed-2025-credits-and-exclusions]]
- **Transfer taxes:** estate and gift — see [[us-fed-2025-estate-and-gift]]
- **Legacy effects:** TCJA 2017 — see [[tcja-2017-effects]]
