# Estate and Gift Tax — US Federal, Tax Year 2025

> **Jurisdiction:** US-FED · **Tax year:** 2025

| Item | 2025 amount | Change vs 2024 |
|---|---:|---:|
| Estate tax basic exclusion | $13,990,000 | +$380,000 |
| Annual gift exclusion | $19,000 | +$1,000 |

The estate basic exclusion is the amount that passes free of federal estate tax for decedents dying in 2025. The annual gift exclusion is the per-donee amount that can be gifted each calendar year without filing a gift-tax return.
